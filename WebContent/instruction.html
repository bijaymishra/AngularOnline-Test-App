Instructions for Taking a Test

Taking the Test

  1. Select an answer for every question. Unanswered questions will be scored as incorrect.

  2. There are currently only one possible question types:

    • Single Choice: click the radio button to indicate your choice. Currently, only one answer can be selected for a multiple choice question.

    • If you use a wheel button mouse, take care not to accidentally change your answers. Sometimes scrolling the wheel will rotate through the answers in a selection list, when you might have meant simply to scroll farther down in the Test window.

  3. Click on the Submit Answers button at the bottom of the page to have your answers graded and successfully logout.

  4. You will be shown your results, including your score and any feedback offered by the author of the Test. You might wish to print this page for your own records. At this stage, you might be able to check your answers: see below.

  5. Close this browser window when you are done submitting the answers.

  6. The author of the Test may have helpful comments for each question. Be sure to check for this feedback.
